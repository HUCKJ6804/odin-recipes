Honey Chicken

Description

Asian - Intermediate

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast -1 Pound (3 pieces)
  • Flour -5 cups
  • Buttermilk - 1 Quart
  • Rice - 3 cups
  • Water - 4 cups
  • Canola Oil - 1 Quart

Sauce Ingredients

  • Cornstarch - 1 Tablespoon
  • Honey - 2/3 cup
  • Soy Sauce - 1 Tablespoon
  • Apple Cider Vinegar - 1 Tablespoon
  • Sesame Oil - 1 Teaspoon

Steps

IMPORTANT take out chicken a day before so it can dethaw by the day you want to make this meal!

    Preparing the Chicken 1-1

  1. Take Chicken Breast and cut them into 1 inch squares.
  2. Drain any juices that may have come out.
  3. Take 1 cup of Flour and pour over the chicken.
  4. Cover the flour over the chicken until all are covered.
  5. Take the Buttermilk and pour over the flour covered chicken and let it marinate for a few minutes.

    6. Preparing the Canola Oil

    1. Take a large pot and place it onto a large stove top range.
    2. Pour entire Canola Oil into it.
    3. Cover with lid.
    4. Turn the heat to 7.

    Preparing the Rice

      These steps are if you have an IPOT if you don't have one, that's alright, follow the instructs as you would, with a normal pot.

    1. Pour the 3 cups of Rice into the IPOT
    2. Do the same thing with the 4 cups of Water.
    3. Press the Rice setting.

      4. The entire process for the IPOT to come to pressure, cook, and decompress is around 20 minutes total.

    4. Once the IPOT has decompressed, open the lid and with a fork, fluff up the rice and turn off the IPOT.

    Preparing the Chicken 1.2

  6. Pull out a new large bowl. Put about a 3rd of the buttermilk covered chicken into the bowl.
  7. Pour 1 cup of Flour.
  8. Repeat steps 14 and 15 until all the chicken is in the new bowl with Flour covering it.
  9. Cover the large bowl and shake for a few minutes to get the chicken covered with the flour.

    10. Cooking the Chicken

    1. Take the Canola oil filled pot's lid off, and place as many pieces of chicken into the oil.

      2. Note The oil will begin to frying the chicken, so beware of splattering!

    2. After around 7 minutes, or when the chicken has fried to a golden brown, remove from the pot and place it onto a plate with a few papertowels to help soak up any excess oil.
    3. Repeat step 19 until all chicken has been fried.

Preparing the Honey Sauce

  1. Take a medium sized pot and add all of the Sauce Ingredients to it.
  2. Place on medium heat. Stirring occassionally.
  3. When the sauce begins to boil and bubble, stir frequently for about 5 minutes.
  4. After the 5 minutes, remove the pan from heat.

    5. Final Preparation

    1. Take the fried chicken and place it into a large bowl. Take the still hot Honey Sauce and pour over the chicken.
    2. Stir occassionally for about 5 minutes as the Honey Sauce cools down.
    3. Take a bowl, and put a few scoops of Rice and add some Soy Sauce to it.
    4. Take the Honey Chicken and place on top of the rice.

Enjoy!
